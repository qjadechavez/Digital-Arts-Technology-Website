ABOUT US

Digital Arts Technology

Digital Arts Technology Training Center Inc., had recently established in June 2021.

The Institution is registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission with its Primary purpose: to operate as an Educational Institution which provides technical skills and vocational programs includeing specialized short courses, training, reviews, and National Certification.

The Institution shall endeavor to achieve the satisfaction of its various trainings and services to its stakeholders.

Vission

“To be the leading Institution that provides world-class technical skills and quality training in the latest trends in Digital Arts and Information Technology”

Mission

“To teach and train students to become globally competitive individual.”

Objective

Digital Arts Technology TC Inc, has its own objective and aims to:

  • Provide quality training to its students using the latest trends and technology in Computer Programming Digital Animation and Computer Systems Servicing;
  • Prepare students to become globally competitive in their field of expertise and specialization;
  • Respond to the increasing needs of global Industry in Information Technology, Innovations, Programming, and Digital Animation.
